About Me

Why I Coach Behavior Change—For Parents, Traders, and Anyone Ready for Real Growth

Hey, I’m Terrell. For 13 years, I was deep in two worlds: day trading and personal training. I’ve seen firsthand how the same patterns—self-doubt, discipline struggles, and emotional habits—show up whether you’re in the gym or at the trading desk.

Most of my fitness clients were parents, and over time, I realized that it wasn’t just the workouts or meal plans that held people back. It was behaviors: the guilt, the time pressure, the feeling of “I should be doing better.” The same was true in trading—no matter how much I knew about the markets, it was my mindset and habits that made or broke my results.

That’s why I became a NASM-certified Behavioral Change Specialist. My passion is helping people—parents, traders, anyone who feels stuck—finally break those old cycles and build habits that last. I coach from experience, not just theory. I know what it’s like to feel overwhelmed, to start over, to want real change but not know where to start.

My approach is honest, non-judgmental, and 100% tailored to you. We’ll work together to uncover what’s really getting in your way and build a plan that fits your life. No quick fixes—just real talk, real support, and real progress.If you’re ready to move past old patterns and start seeing changes that stick, you’re in the right place. Let’s get to work—together.

What the Logos Mean

A Holistic Approach to Change

My main logo brings together the owl, phoenix, and fox—three symbols that represent the heart of my coaching. Change isn’t one-size-fits-all. It’s about wisdom, resilience, and adaptability working together, no matter what life throws at you. This is the foundation for every client I work with, whether you’re a parent, trader, or just ready for something new.

The Phoenix: Resilience & Renewal

The phoenix stands for rising from setbacks—again and again. I use this symbol for my fitness clients and anyone who’s been knocked down by old habits or tough circumstances. It’s about starting fresh, building strength, and believing you can come back stronger—every single time.

The Owl: Wisdom for Parents

The owl is a symbol of wisdom, patience, and seeing the bigger picture. For parents, this means breaking generational patterns, finding calm in the chaos, and trusting yourself—even when parenting feels impossible. My work with parents is about helping you tap into your own wisdom and lead your family with confidence.

The Fox: Adaptability for Traders

The fox represents adaptability, quick thinking, and staying sharp—qualities every trader and investor needs. In coaching traders, I focus on helping you pivot, stay disciplined, and outsmart old habits that can sabotage your success. It’s about being flexible and always ready for the next move.
